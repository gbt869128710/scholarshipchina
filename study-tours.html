Study Tours

With the growth of its economy, international students from different parts of the world come to study in China so as to deeply learn more about China. While, for those who have never come to China before, a study tour for 1-2 weeks in a Chinese university will be a good choice. Thus, ScholarshipChina has contacted several Chinese universities for short-term study & travel tours, and has collected all the program information for everyone. Just get your understanding about China from your first study tour.
