Guidance For Pickup Service

After getting your scholarships and visa done successfully, it’s time for you to prepare to come to China. As China covers a very large land and each province or city is diverse from each other, it’s sometimes very troublesome for international students to find cars and go to the school for registration. Now, ScholarshipChina will clear all these worries and provides an easy journey for you from airport/train station to school.

Service Price: 600rmb

Service contents:

1)Airport/Train station pickup
2)Car service to school
3)Registration service in school
4)School introduction and orientation

Booking procedures

  • Step 1: Login your ScholarshipChina account and click “Airport pickup service”
  • Step 2: Complete your Airport pickup form
  • Step 3: Pay for the fees
  • Step 4: Get confirmation email within one working day
  • Step 5: Get picked up and register in school
  • Step 6: Leave a comment for this pickup on our website

* When arriving in the school after finishing the registration process, they will guide you for a visit in your school and introduce different parts of the school including teaching buildings, library, sports facilities, shops, restaurants etc. Besides, they will also help find you the nearest money exchange ATM or bank, post offices, pharmacies and recommended hospitals, and a simple travel guide about the school.
