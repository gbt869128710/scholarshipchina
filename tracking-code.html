Scholarshipchina Tracking Code

After sending your admission paper and jw202 out, ScholarshipChina will give you an express tracking code so that you can check the shipping information everyday.

Domestic Express Delivery

Delivery fee is on ScholarshipChina except SF express
For domestic express delivery, ScholarshipChina has a connection with EMS. But if you want to SF express in fast speed, delivery fee will be on your side.

Aboard Express Delivery

Delivery fee is on ScholarshipChina
For express delivery aboard , ScholarshipChina has a connection with TNT. Normally, 4-8 working days, you will receive your docs. But if your post address was not in TNT system, ScholarshipChina will send your docs out by DHL.

FAQ

How long will i receive my docs?

For EMS, normally 2-5 days, you will receive the docs.
For SF, normally 1-4 days, you will receive the docs.
For TNT and DHL, 6-13 days, you will receive the docs.

What if i wrote the post infomation wrong?

Before sending it out, ScholarshipChina will check the post information with you again. If the wrong information is found after sending it out, ScholarshipChina will contact the customer service at once.

What if my express delayed?

Domestic express delivery
ScholarhipChina will help you call the customer service or give you the number of the delivery man so that you can contact him by yourself.

Aboard express delivery
ScholarhipChina will help you call the customer service and ask for the season. What’s more, a local TNT customer service telephone will be given so that you can contact them.

Do i have to contact ScholarshipChina after receiving my docs?

Upon receiving your docs, it will be very much appreciated if you can take a photo with your Admission notice and Jw202 and send your photo or video to info@scholarshipchina.com.
