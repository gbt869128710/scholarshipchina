    Who We Are

    At ScholarshipChina, our goal is to make it easier for students to find information and secure self-financed placement and scholarships in Chinese universities. We strive to improve the visibility of the Chinese universities on the global marketplace.

    We realize that trying to find information about universities in China can be a stressful ordeal not made any easier by the language barrier. In order to satisfy the needs of the students therefore, we provide an extensive database of scholarships that are available to international students.

    What We Do

    Students using our service get a chance to locate the type of scholarships suitable for them. Selections can be based on course duration, field of study, as well as and degree. ScholarshipChina provides unbiased information about the scholarships including the universities, the accommodation and types of courses that students can select for the duration of their study.

    In addition, we give a general overview of the different cities in China to help the students get an idea of the sort of area they shall be calling home as they study, be it for a short period of time or for a long time.

    FAQ

    What if my application failed, can I get my fee back?

    Our fee structure is divided into 2 parts: 1. Commitment fee 2. Service fee( Most universities will charge you some fees as university application fee, to make JW202 and help you register). Commitment fee and university application fee are non-refundable. Service fee can be refunded when the university can’t accept the students for some reason(such as can’t issue JW202). However, if the student doesn't get the scholarship because of personal mistakes, such as he/she doesn't go to the university on time, our service fee will not be refunded.

    I don't know what major to choose. Any suggestion?

    Generally, there are 3 kinds of majors. First one is Liberal Arts, which covers the subjects like Law, Literature and so on. Second one is engineering related, such as civil engineering, chemical engineering or mining engineering. The third one is science related, such as physics, Chemistry.

    You need to think about what is your interested majors. Also, think about what is your job prospect in the future. Of course, if you really don’t have any idea in which major to choose, you can take one-year Chinese Language. Firstly, good Chinese is a huge advantage in studying in China. Secondly, during this time, you can also consider about what major you want while enjoying your life in China. Thirdly, it is cost-saving, so why not?

    If I can't arrive at school on time, will the accommodation be available?

    At every university the seats for accommodation are limited, but there are lots of international students to enroll. If you can’t arrive at school for a long time, accommodation may not be available. Besides, if you arrive at school very early, the university will not provide accommodation and registration service. So please arrive at school on the designated time.

    Can I choose the dormitory by myself?

    The dormitory will be arranged by the university. You can’t choose the dormitory by yourself. And dormitory’s photos are for reference only.

    I'm studying in China now, can I transfer to another school?

    Yes, but you need to start from Freshman Year. And we need your transfer certificate from the school you are studying now. Besides, if you want to apply for bachelor’s degree, your age should be under 30 years old. Age is better below 35 years old, if you want to apply for master’s degree. For PhD’s degree, age should be under 40 years old.
