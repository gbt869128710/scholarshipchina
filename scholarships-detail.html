Procedure

  • Choose one scholarship program you are going to apply
  • Fulfill out the applicant’s information and upload documents required online
  • Pay the application fee
  • Sign up the scholarship application
  • The admission notice will be out around 10days (exact duration depends on program)
  • Pay the university’s registration fee or deposit (charged by university, some universities require students to pay the university’s registration fee or deposit after admission notice comes out)
  • JW202 will be issued around 3 weeks (exact duration depends on program)
  • Pay the program fee
  • The original documents will be sent you within 2days
  • Apply for VISA with documents required by embassy
  • Book the air ticket to China and register in the university
  • Enjoy your China Study Tour

Application Documents

  • Scanned copy of your passport
  • One page of blank copy of your passport
  • Visa page (if available)
  • Notarized highest diploma
  • Academic transcripts
  • Two Recommendation Letters（only for master & PhD）
  • Medical check
  • Study plan（only for master & PhD）
  • CV
  • Digital Passport-size photo
  • Some universities need noun criminal certificate, bank statement, better prepare it in advance.
    *All documents should be in English or in Chinese
